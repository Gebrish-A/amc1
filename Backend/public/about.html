Our Journey

Founded in 2025 within Amhara Media Corporation, our CMS began as an internal solution to streamline complex media workflows. What started as a departmental project quickly evolved into a comprehensive platform serving media organizations across Ethiopia.

Our team of software developers, database administrators, and cybersecurity experts combined their skills to create a system that addresses the unique challenges of media management in today's digital landscape.
